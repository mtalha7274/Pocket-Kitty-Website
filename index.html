Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

Welcome to Pocket Kitty ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

  • Account Information: Name, email, and profile picture via Google/Apple SSO.
  • User Content: Posts, comments, and media uploaded to the community feed.
  • Pet Information: Names, breeds, and photos of pets.
  • Device Data: Tokens for push notifications and usage logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide the service, facilitate sharing, send activity notifications, and moderate content using AI to ensure safety.

3. Third Party Services

Pocket Kitty uses the following third party services that may collect and process your personal information as part of providing authentication and core functionality.

Google Sign-In: We use Google Sign-In to allow you to create an account and log in securely. By using Google Sign-In, your authentication is handled by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple Sign-In: We use Sign in with Apple to allow you to create an account and log in securely. By using Sign in with Apple, your authentication is handled by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

These services only receive the minimum information necessary to authenticate your identity. We do not share your personal data with any advertising networks or data brokers.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your personal data including your profile, posts, comments, and pet information will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You may request account deletion at any time through the app settings or by contacting us at support@pocketmicros.com.

5. Children's Privacy

Pocket Kitty is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at support@pocketmicros.com and we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use or access Pocket Kitty.

6. Data Deletion

You retain the right to delete your data. You may request account deletion directly within the app settings or by contacting support.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@pocketmicros.com.
